Hiking is an activity of moderate difficulty, which involves walking across long distances generally on trails or paths. The duration of the activity varies between short half-day programs and longer itineraries of over 20 days. It is usually an activity that allows groups of different sizes.

WALK & HIKE

There's no better way to unlock the secrets of the High Country than on your own two feet. Mt Buller and neighbouring Mt Stirling offer a variety of self-guided and guided hiking experiences for all ages and levels of fitness.

Hiking is an activity available most of the year, depending on the area. Winter is not a preferred season for hiking programs, as it can be harsher and more dangerous. Most mountain areas where ski sports are practiced during the winter are suitable for hiking programs on the warmer months.
Hiking is not just walking around. This is why the role of mountain guides is essential to a safe and interesting hiking experience. They will know the state of the different trails and paths, set up the best itineraries, and determine how safe each of them is. This includes knowledge about the flora and fauna in the area, how to engage with it and respect it.
